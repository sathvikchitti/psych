What this chart shows

This graph plots your ManoDhwani probability score across each assessment session over time. Each dot represents one day's result — green for low risk (<52%), amber for moderate (52–61%), and red for high risk (≥62%). Tracking these scores week over week helps you and your clinician observe whether symptoms are improving, stable, or escalating — making it a key tool for longitudinal mental health monitoring.
